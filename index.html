Biography

Wale Akinfaderin is a Physicist and an Insight Data Science Fellow with almost a decade experience in interdisciplinary scientific research and computational intelligence. He is currently a PhD Candidate in Physics and a Graduate Researcher at the National High Magnetic Field Laboratory, Florida State University (FSU). His research focuses on the development of Overhauser Dynamic Nuclear Polarization at High Magnetic Field.

In the past, he has built data veracity model using machine learning and natural language processing techniques for international development with IBM Research. He is a machine learning proposal reviewer for Cambridge University Press, a mentor for Data Science Nigeria and the organizer of Data Science Tallahassee meetup group. He’s a recipient of Charles and Anna Uhrhan Scholarship for Outstanding PhD Students in Physics, Golden Key Research Grant Award and a NASA & NSF-CREST Center Scholarship in Physics.
